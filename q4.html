Comparison of PNG and JPG Images

Pros of PNG Images

  • Lossless compression
  • Supports transparency
  • Great for logos and sharp edges
  • Retains quality after edits
  • Widely supported

Cons of PNG Images

  • Larger file sizes
  • Not ideal for photos
  • Less efficient compression
  • Can be slow to load
  • File size increases with resolution

Pros of JPG Images

  • Smaller file sizes
  • Great for photos
  • Efficient compression
  • Faster loading times
  • Good balance of quality and size

Cons of JPG Images

  • Lossy compression
  • No transparency support
  • Quality degrades with edits
  • Artifacts with heavy compression
  • Not good for text or sharp edges
